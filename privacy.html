Privacy Policy

For the Discord bot The Host. Last updated: 17 June 2026.

The Host is a community game bot for Discord. This policy explains what data it stores, why, and how you can have it removed. We try to keep this plain and honest rather than full of legalese.

What we store

The Host stores the minimum needed to run its games and economy. Specifically:

  • Your Discord user ID — the numeric ID Discord assigns you, used to keep track of your account in the bot.
  • Your display name — cached so leaderboards and messages can show a readable name.
  • In-game currency balances — your Shards and Coolios totals, and lifetime earned totals used for ranking.
  • Stock holdings & trade history — which in-game stocks you own, and a log of your buys and sells (used for profit/loss tracking).
  • Gift & transaction logs — records of in-game gifts and currency changes, used to enforce daily limits and show history.
  • Game state — things like flair, betting entries, and any price alerts or orders you set.
  • Message activity counts — the bot counts how many messages occur (as a number) to drive an in-game "server activity" stat. It does not store the content of your messages.

What we do NOT store

  • We do not store the content of your messages, DMs, or voice activity.
  • We do not collect email addresses, real names, IP addresses, or payment information.
  • All "currency" is fictional in-game points with no real-world value.

Why we store it

Purely to make the games work — to remember your balances between sessions, run the economy and stock market, show leaderboards, and enforce fair-play limits. Your data is not sold, shared, or used for advertising.

Where it's stored

Data is kept in local databases on the private server that runs the bot. It is not shared with third parties. The bot operates across a small number of affiliated Discord servers that share one game economy.

Your rights — viewing and deleting your data

You can request a copy of the data the bot holds about you, or ask for it to be deleted, at any time:

  • /exportmydata — sends you a copy of your stored data in a direct message.
  • /deletemydata — permanently erases your records from the bot.

You may also ask a server administrator to do this for you. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone; you'll lose any balances, holdings, and history.

Data retention

We keep your data as long as you use the bot. If you leave all servers the bot is in, or request deletion, your data is removed. Some logs may persist briefly in backups before aging out.

Children

This bot is intended for use in accordance with Discord's Terms of Service, which require users to meet a minimum age. It is not directed at children under that age.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above will change. Continued use of the bot means you accept the current version.

Contact

Questions about your data can be directed to the bot operator through the Discord server you use the bot in.

The Host is a fan-made community bot and is not affiliated with Discord Inc. or Innersloth (Among Us).
